Profiles

Become a part of the community!

Every JamPad account has a profile, which is uniquely identifiable by their username. Their profile will also display their chosen display name, bio, and follower/following counts. Click on either of these to see the full list of users. Follow a user to see their activity in app and any events they post.

On each basic profile is a list of the user's RSVPed events, separated into upcoming and past. Upcoming events are ordered with the soonest first, descending further into the future.


Past Events

Instead of the normal event information prevented on an event preview, past events instead show their rating. If the profile you're looking at has posted a rating for that event, that will show up here (including any of your own ratings). Otherwise the average event rating will appear here (if at least one rating has been posted).
