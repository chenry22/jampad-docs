Admin Accounts

JamPad admin profiles are marked with a yellow seal.

Admin accounts are active community members who regularly post and verify events happening in their location. They also have the power to verify artists, delete fake events, and regulate event comments and ratings.


Join the Team

There is not a standardized way to request to become a JamPad admin. If you are interested in helping out, reach out to us on Instagram (@jampadapp) or by email (jampadapp@gmail.com). If you are already an established JamPad user and post events, you will be more likely to be accepted as an admin.
