Venue Accounts

Venue accounts are marked with a green seal. A venue account signifies that this user manages the venue listed.

Any events a venue is confirmed to be hosting will show up in a Hosting tab on their profile! This will show both past and upcoming events.


Getting Verified

If you want an venue account, you can go to the Settings page on your profile to get verified. Here you will see a Request Verification button- click this and fill out the form that you are presented with. If you have a pending verification, you will see that here instead.


Venue Account Features

As a verified venue, you have a few special features you have access to. First, any event you post that takes place at your venue will be automatically verified.

You will only be automatically added to verified events created by admins. If a normal JamPad user wants to link your account to an event, they have to send you an event request. You can view these in the Notifications screen on your profile under Event Requests.

Venue Collabs

If you are a venue and you want to collaborate with us, please reach out! This could mean adding merch into the app as frog clothing, setting up a custom reward code, or more!
